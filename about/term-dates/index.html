Term dates

Current term dates

2022/2023

  • Autumn Term: 26 September 2022 - 2 December 2022
  • Spring Term: 9 January 2023 - 17 March 2023
  • Summer Term (undergraduate): 17 April 2023 - 23 June 2023
  • Summer Term (taught postgraduate): 17 April 2023 - 22 September 2023

New students

New students (both undergraduate and taught postgraduate) normally need to arrive before the beginning of term to take part in orientation activities. Some courses start on dates earlier than listed. Please check with your department for details.

Find out more about starting your studies at York

Postgraduates

Taught postgraduate students are required to be in attendance over the summer period.

Postgraduate research students are not ruled by the teaching term dates. However, the availability of your supervisor, plus some services and academic activities, may be reduced outside these dates.

Changes to academic year structure

From September 2023, the University academic year will be split into two semesters and for the majority of courses this will replace our current three term model.

Future semester dates are provided below.

Find out more about semesterisation.
